About Us

Welcome to Tailored Pieces Ug. We are a the leader in both traditional African outfits and urban fashion styles in Makindye, Luwafu.
We offer both rental and custom outfits for all ages, both men and women.
You also will find dresses and accessories full of whimsy, femininity, and charm. Dresses are perfect for flower girls, photography, and so much more
It was established by on February 20th ,2019 by Marion Nansibo.

Our Mission:

To become the most pretigious and desirable luxury goods company by delivering the best quality and style

Our Vision:

The brand,s vision is being timeless and sophisticated. This highlights that the company designs clothing
and offers accesories that are always on trend. i.e. Our products never go out of fashion.

Our Values:

  • Be creative and innovative
  • Deliver Excellence
  • Cultivate an entrepreneural spirit
  • Be committed to a positive impact
